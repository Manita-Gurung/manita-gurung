Some of my movie recommendation.

Here are some of my movie recommendation which are my favourite movies. I hope you all enjoyy! Plastic Memories

If you're looking for an anime that blends heartwarming moments with deep emotional storytelling, I highly recommend Plastic Memories. This series is set in a world where humanoid robots, known as Giftias, live alongside humans, but their lifespans are limited. The story follows Tsukasa, a new employee at the Terminal Service, and Isla, a Giftia nearing the end of her lifespan. As they work together to collect Giftias whose time has run out, the anime explores themes of love, loss, and the fleeting nature of life.

Plastic Memories offers a perfect balance of touching moments, bittersweet romance, and thought-provoking questions about what it means to live and love. If you're a fan of emotional stories with a sci-fi twist, this anime will leave a lasting impact on you.

86

f you're looking for an anime that combines intense action, thought-provoking themes, and deeply emotional storytelling, I highly recommend 86. Set in a dystopian future, the story follows the conflict between the Republic of San Magnolia and the mysterious Legion forces, with a focus on the 86, a group of soldiers who are treated as expendable despite their courage and humanity.

The series explores themes of war, discrimination, and the human cost of conflict, while also highlighting the bond between the main characters, Shin and Lena, who strive to bridge the gap between the 86 and the rest of society. 86 offers a gripping narrative filled with tension and moments of profound emotional depth, making it a must-watch for anyone interested in anime that challenges moral boundaries while delivering thrilling action.
